Reading data from file:
1. Read raw bytes:
    buf := make([]byte, 16)
    f.read(buf)

2. Buffered Reading:
    reader := bufio.NewReader(f)
    reader.Read() : raw bytes
    reader.ReadString()

3. Higher level text interface:
    scanner := bufio.NewScanner(f)
    scanner.Scan(): scanner.Text()
